Electronics Experience

I recently began learning about analog electronics and I hope to try and incorporate that into my software development career. My main area of interest in analog electronics is Synth DIY and the Eurorack Standard. The main project I am working on is a Eurorack compatable music device featuring several DIY Eurorack compliant modules such as a power supply, Voltage Controlled Oscillator (VCO), an 8 beat sequencer, and a Voltage Controlled Filter (VCF).
